Commercial Real Estate Services
FIND’s commercial real estate team understands the unique challenges and opportunities in today’s market. Whether you’re looking to lease office space, acquire a retail location, or develop an investment property, we offer the expertise, local knowledge, and innovative approach to help your business thrive.

Our Services

Office Leasing
FIND helps companies secure the right office space—quickly and confidently. From subleases to long-term leases, we handle the search, negotiation, and paperwork so you can stay focused on growing your business.
Retail Spaces
Location matters in retail. We work with you to find high-traffic, strategically positioned properties to enhance your business’s visibility and customer engagement.
Industrial & Warehouse Properties
Our team has extensive experience in industrial real estate, helping you navigate complex logistics and site requirements for warehouses, distribution centers, and more.
Investment Properties
Looking to expand your portfolio? Our experts provide insights into market trends, property valuations, and high-potential investment opportunities to maximize your returns.
Development & Land Acquisition
From land acquisition to property development, FIND supports you in every stage of the development process, turning your vision into a profitable reality.
